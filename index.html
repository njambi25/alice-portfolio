About

I moved from Nairobi to San Diego by way of expo-stand 3D design, WordPress builds, and brand identity work — every stop added a layer to how I think about design: physical space, digital space, and now the behavior layer that AI adds on top of both.

My freelance practice has always sat at the intersection of brand and web — logo through launch, for clients who had never had a designer before. What's changed in the last year is the ceiling on what "the interface" even means: on Sincera, part of my job was designing how an AI coach talks, not just how a screen looks.

I'm looking for a Product Designer role where that combination — visual craft, product thinking, and genuine fluency directing AI tools — is the point, not a side skill.
